San Andreas Army has finally received funding

GTA San Andreas Army was created in the early 2004 and hasn't received a single funding project ever since until recently. The government has decided it needed new uniforms, since the old ones where all worn out.

New major plans for the weaponry are planned for the GTASA Army as well. New guns with modern red-dot scopes are expected to be bought up until 2020.
