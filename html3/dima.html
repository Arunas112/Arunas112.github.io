Dima's opinion on army

Army has changed my life upside down. I have met a lot of great people, they have taught me how to use guns so I can shoot bad people! I am also even stronger and scarier (especially when I have a gun in my hands) than I used to be.

I am getting used to the climate and people out there and I am having a lot of fun. If you will someday get recruited you are blessed to have an opportunity to come there and serve for your country!
